# Projects
## Quick pong
"Quick Pong" is a single-player arcade game inspired by the timeless classic, Pong. It aims to deliver a fast-paced, skill-based experience by introducing two core mechanics that elevate the challenge and replayability. The unique selling proposition (USP) lies in its progressively increasing ball speed with each paddle hit, which resets upon scoring, and a dynamic ball deflection system where the angle of rebound is determined by the exact point of contact on the paddle. This creates a more nuanced and demanding gameplay loop than its predecessor, focusing on precision and quick reflexes.

## Apple collector
Apple Collector is a fast-paced, modern arcade game that reimagines the classic snake formula. Players control a nimble character on a single screen, tasked with collecting apples while navigating a field of perpetually moving obstacles.  unlike classic collection games, you don't grow longer or become slower; instead, a single mistake costs you your entire hoard.

## Mist realm
Mist Realm (Primary Title) is a 2D side-scrolling action RPG that blends tight, skill-based combat with deep environmental storytelling and a unique fantasy narrative. Players assume the role of Elara, the "Child of Alabaster," a white-haired outcast who must traverse a series of mystical realms connected by a primordial mist. The game's USP is its deeply integrated mist-based traversal and combat system, where the environment is both a puzzle and a weapon, and its narrative where each realm reflects an emotional state, directly influencing both the story and gameplay mechanics. As Elara masters the mist, she uncovers the truth of her destiny and challenges the technologically advanced Vulari Empire.
