# Relay rush
Relay Rush is a 2D top-down action-adventure game set in a world devastated by interplanetary war. Play as C-55, a courier robot and deliver vital packages across dangerous territories, explore diverse tile-based zones, gather valuable resources, and overcome hazards and enemies on every mission. Upgrade your capabilities, take on greater challenges, and become the link that holds civilization together.
